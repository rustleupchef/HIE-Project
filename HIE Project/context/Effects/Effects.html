Greenhouse gas emission

The Burning of fossil fuels causes the release of greenhouse gasses. This causes air pollution, ocean acidification, and  global warming. This is bad because it affects all species, not just us, and this might all lead to a tipping point where everything goes catastrophic.

I want to quickly go over why these effects are bad. Ocean acidification can cause the killing of many ocean lives which endanger many people who rely on that food. Air pollution is bad for every species, and can cause the sickening of all lives, and finally global warming which can cause sea levels to rise, threats to biodiversity, possible increase in hurricanes, and negative impact on biolife.

The only upside is that they are extremely cheap, so to counter this we are going to need a new source of energy that produces a lot for the cost.

Now going onto why it matters so much. It matters so much because the top impacts of our environments all come back to fossil fuels, so If the ideas I have brought up are brought to life then we can counter almost all the issues that lead to this global concern.
